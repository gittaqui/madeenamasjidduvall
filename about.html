About Madeena Masjid Duvall

Allah Help Those Who Help Themselves

We are a welcoming Muslim community in Duvall dedicated to worship, education, and service—nurturing faith, character, and compassionate action for all ages.

Our Vision

To be a welcoming house of worship that nurtures faith, character, and service for the Duvall community.

Our Mission

Establish daily prayers, offer Maktab for kids and Hifz programs, provide Islamic education for all ages, and serve our neighbors through compassion and outreach.
