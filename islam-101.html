An Introduction to Islam

Core Beliefs

  • Belief in one God (Allah)
  • Belief in the Prophets and the final Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him)
  • Belief in the revealed books, angels, the Day of Judgment, and divine decree

The Five Pillars

  • Shahada: Declaration of faith
  • Salah: Daily prayers
  • Zakat: Almsgiving
  • Sawm: Fasting in Ramadan
  • Hajj: Pilgrimage to Mecca
